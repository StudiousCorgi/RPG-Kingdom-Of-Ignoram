World Setting

Long ago, the Kingdom of Ignoram was a place of brilliance, where libraries soared like castles and every child was a hero of learning. But one day, King Ignoramous rose to power. Jealous of others' knowledge and afraid of questions, he declared learning dangerous. He shut down schools, burned books (but only the confusing ones), and replaced puzzles with coloring inside the lines.
Now, most grown-ups shuffle through life without curiosity. But the spark of wonder still glows in the hearts of children. When twin sisters, Misha and Lexi, sneak into the Forbidden Forest to chase a legend, they discover a hidden cave filled with glowing scrolls, mechanical owls, and magical chalkboards.
