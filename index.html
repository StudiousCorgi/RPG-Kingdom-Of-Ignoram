Kingdom of Ignoram - Kid-Friendly RPG

World Setting

The Kingdom of Ignoram is ruled by the evil King Ignoramous, who has outlawed all learning! Reading, writing, and even thinking too much is considered a crime. Children suffer the most, stuck in hard-labor roles forever... until hope returns.

Twin girls find a hidden cave and a forgotten teacher. They are gifted the magical tools of rebellion: the Fairy Tale Wand (Reading Magic) and the Sword of Statistics (Math Magic). Their mission? Educate the kingdom, one encounter at a time!
