The Kingdom of Ignoram

Legend

  • 🏰 Cities – Major settlements where trade and knowledge once flourished.
  • ⚓ Coastal Ports – Cities that sit by the sea, once used for trade and secret travel.
  • 🌊 River of Riddles – The lifeblood of the land, flowing north from the ocean, spawning lakes along the way.
  • 🕳️ Dungeons – Dangerous places filled with lost knowledge and twisted creatures.
  • ⛏️ Mines – Labor zones where children toil under the King’s rule.
  • 🏚️ Ruins – Former strongholds of wisdom, now silent.

Key Locations & Lore

  • 📍 Lexmoor (C4): A coastal stronghold city, formerly home to a great university. Now used as a command post for King Ignoramous's inquisitors.
  • 📍 Port Scribble (E2): A lively port town where legends say secret books still flow through black market trade.
  • 📍 Quillspire (F6): Nestled in the central valley by a lake, this city holds the last hidden library, sealed by enchanted riddles.
  • 📍 The Riddle Delta (B2): Where the River of Riddles meets the sea. Said to be guarded by ancient sea spirits who test travelers with logic puzzles.
  • 📍 Crag of Forgotten Thoughts (D5): An eerie ruin perched on a cliff. Glowing runes pulse here on moonless nights.
  • 📍 The Mindshaft Mines (G4): A massive complex of child-operated mining tunnels. Secret tunnels lead to lost underground classrooms.
  • 📍 Hollow Hollow (E5): A vast dungeon within a sinkhole. Known for shape-shifting riddles that drive invaders mad.
