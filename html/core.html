Core Mechanics

🎲 Core Mechanics

d20 Knowledge Checks

Roll a d20 when your character tries something big—solving puzzles, using magic, escaping traps.

  • +2 if it's related to their Role or Skill
  • +1 to +3 for creative ideas or magic item use

Difficulty Levels:

  • Easy = 7+
  • Medium = 10+
  • Hard = 15+
  • Boss = 18+

Failure never hurts—just delays or creates funny complications!

📚 Learning Magic

Magic is powered by real knowledge—reading, math, logic, or memory.

  • Fairy Tale Wand: Casts word-based spells when a player reads something aloud
  • Sword of Statistics: Casts number-based spells by solving a math problem

Players can invent their own spells too!

📈 Knowledge Points (KP)

Instead of XP, players earn KP by:

  • Solving puzzles
  • Reading or writing in-game
  • Teaching someone something new
  • Turning an enemy into a friend through learning

Spend KP to:

  • Level up
  • Gain new powers
  • Upgrade your magical items
  • Trigger "Group Discoveries" for the whole team

🧩 Bonuses Systems

  • Brain Boost: If a player does something super clever, let them roll twice and pick the better result
  • Puzzles & Teaching: Mini-games and riddles bring learning into the world (decoding maps, rewiring gadgets, fixing broken books)
  • Safe Failures: Failing a roll may trigger funny mishaps, like "summoning glitter bees" or "accidentally charming a chair."
